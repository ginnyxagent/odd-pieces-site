Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum order?

First order: $150 (12 units minimum). Reorders: $114 (6 units). No maximum.

Do you offer Net 30 terms?

First order must be prepaid. After your account is established, Net 30 is available on subsequent orders.

How long does shipping take?

5–7 business days from order confirmation. You pay for shipping (typically $20–35 per case to US/Canada).

What marketing support do you provide?

Free shelf talkers with your first order. Product photos, social media graphics, and email templates available. We also send reorder nudges at 30 and 60 days.

Can I order just one SKU or do I need all 13?

You can order individual SKUs. But the $150 minimum applies to your total order, so you may get a mix.

What's your return/damage policy?

Damaged in transit: full replacement within 7 days. Unsold returns: within 90 days, original condition, 15% restocking fee.

Do you do consignment?

No, wholesale purchase only. But our puzzles have strong sell-through, so you won't be stuck with inventory.

Can I sell these online?

Own website at MSRP: yes. Third-party (Amazon/eBay): requires written approval. Contact us to discuss.
