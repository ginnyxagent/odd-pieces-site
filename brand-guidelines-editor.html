Your edits will be saved and the live brand guidelines updated. Here's how publishing works:

  1. Click Download Updated File — saves the new brand-guidelines-v6.html
  2. Move it to your odd-pieces-site folder (replace the existing file)
  3. Run vercel deploy --prod in your terminal
  4. Done — team sees the live updates at the Vercel URL ✓
