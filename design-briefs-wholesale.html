🎨 Wholesale Design Briefs

For Martin • Odd Pieces • March 2026

Brief 1: Price List / Sell Sheet

Overview

What: One-page PDF that retailers see first. This is your sales document — it sells for you when you're not in the room.

Format: Single page, can be front/back or 8.5" x 11" (letter size, can fold to 5.5" x 8.5")

Deliverable: High-res PDF + design file (Figma/Illustrator)

Dimensions & Setup

8.5" × 11" (letter size) 300 dpi (for print) Bleed: 0.25" on all sides Safe zone: 0.5" from edges

SIDE A (Front) - The Pitch

Purpose: Hook the buyer with your unique value prop in 15 seconds

  1. Logo placement (top left)
    • Odd Pieces logo, 1.5" width
    • Leave 0.5" margin from edges
  2. Hero tagline (top, centered)
    • Text: "Mystery Puzzles — Build to Find Out What Happens"
    • Font: Nunito 900, 32pt, uppercase, black (#1A1A1A)
    • Line height: 1.2
    • Max width: 5 inches (leave breathing room)
  3. Hero imagery (centered, 3-4" height)
    • Show 2–3 product shots side-by-side:
    • One: Box cover image (the "before" scene)
    • Two: Completed puzzle photo (the "after" scene)
    • Three: (Optional) A detail shot or SKU example
    • Use high-quality, well-lit product photography
    • White or cream background to stay clean
  4. Three selling bullets (left-aligned, below images)
    • Font: Inter 500, 12pt, black
    • Bullets: small yellow circles (#FEE000) or bold dots
    • Bullet 1: "The box art ≠ the puzzle — every build surprises"
    • Bullet 2: "1000 pieces, premium quality, artist-designed"
    • Bullet 3: "4.8★ from 2,000+ reviews"
    • Line spacing: 1.5
  5. Social proof strip (bottom right, optional)
    • Small badge/badge-style graphic
    • Text: "Kickstarter Funded" + funding amount (e.g., "$XXX,XXX")
    • Or: "500+ 5-star reviews" or "Amazon Bestseller"
    • Font: Inter 600, 10pt, can be white on yellow or black on white

SIDE B (Back) - The Details

Purpose: Give retailers the exact information they need to make a buying decision

  1. Product lineup table (top half, left-aligned)

    Columns: SKU | Product Name | Wholesale | MSRP | Case Pack

    SKU Product Wholesale MSRP Case Pack
    OP-001 [Series 1 Puzzle 1] $19.00 $38.00 12 units
    OP-002 [Series 1 Puzzle 2] $19.00 $38.00 12 units
    OP-003 [Series 1 Puzzle 3] $19.00 $38.00 12 units
    + all other SKUs...

    Design notes:

    • Font: Inter 400, 10pt (body), Inter 600 (header)
    • Header background: #F5F3F0 (light gray)
    • Rows alternating: white + #FFFBF0 (cream)
    • Highlight wholesale column in light yellow (#FFFEF5) or bold the price
    • Black borders, 1pt stroke
  2. Key selling point callout (middle right)
    • Box/banner with yellow (#FEE000) background or border
    • Text: "56% Retailer Margin" or "Retailers earn $21 per puzzle sold"
    • Font: Nunito 700, 14pt, black
    • Subtext (Inter 400, 10pt): "At MSRP $38 with wholesale $17"
  3. Order information (bottom left)
    • Section header: "HOW TO ORDER" (Nunito 700, 12pt, uppercase)
    • Three lines of info (Inter 400, 10pt):
    • • Email: wholesale@oddpieces.com
    • • Website: oddpieces.com/wholesale
    • • Or fill out the online form (link provided)
  4. Payment terms (bottom middle)
    • Section header: "PAYMENT TERMS" (Nunito 700, 12pt, uppercase)
    • Three lines of info (Inter 400, 10pt):
    • • First order: Prepaid
    • • Reorders: Net 30 (after account established)
    • • Ships within 5 business days
  5. Marketing support callout (bottom right)
    • Section header: "YOU GET" (Nunito 700, 12pt, uppercase)
    • Two bullet points (Inter 400, 10pt):
    • ✓ Free shelf talkers with first order
    • ✓ Marketing materials + ongoing support

Brand Guidelines

Colors:

Yellow #FEE000 (primary accent)
Blue #C1E3E8 (secondary)
Black #1A1A1A (text)
Cream #FFFBF0 (background)

Fonts:

  • Headlines: Nunito 900 (uppercase, bold)
  • Subheaders: Nunito 700 (uppercase)
  • Body: Inter 400 (mixed case, readable)
  • Emphasis: Inter 600 (bold text)

Spacing:

  • Margins: 0.5" from page edge
  • Section gaps: 0.25"–0.5"
  • Line height: 1.2 (headlines), 1.6 (body)
🎯 Key Success Metric: A retailer should understand pricing, margin, and how to order in under 60 seconds. Keep it clean, scannable, and obvious.

Brief 2: Shelf Talker / Point-of-Sale Card

Overview

What: Small printed card that sits on retail shelves next to your product. Purpose: explain the mystery concept in 5 seconds, drive impulse purchase.

Format: 3.5" × 2" (business card size) OR 4" × 6" (postcard, folds to stand)

Deliverable: Design file + print-ready PDF (300 dpi, with bleeds)

Design (3.5" × 2" Business Card Size)

  1. Front
    • Logo (top left): Odd Pieces logo, 0.75" width
    • Headline (centered, bold): "The box art isn't what you build." (Nunito 700, 14pt, uppercase)
    • Product image (center): One 2"×2" product photo (box cover or completed puzzle, whichever is more eye-catching)
    • Tagline (bottom): "1000-piece mystery puzzle" (Inter 400, 8pt, centered)
  2. Back (fold down for display)
    • Headline: "How it works:" (Inter 600, 9pt)
    • Three steps (Inter 400, 7pt):
    • • Open the box
    • • Build the puzzle
    • • Discover the surprise
    • Rating (large): "⭐ 4.8/5 from 2,000+ puzzlers" (Inter 600, 8pt, centered, bold)
    • QR code (bottom right): 0.5"×0.5" QR linking to product page

Material & Finish:

  • 14pt cardstock (premium feel, substantial weight)
  • Matte or silk finish (not glossy — feels better in hand)
  • Full-bleed color (no white border)

Alternative: 4" × 6" Postcard (Fold to Stand)

  1. Design layout:
    • Fold horizontally (top folds down to create a standing triangle)
    • Front (visible when standing): Logo + "The box art isn't what you build." + product image
    • Inside (when standing): Headline + "How it works" + rating + QR
    • Back (fold): Can include coupon code (e.g., "First time? Use code WHOLESALE15") or leave blank for retailer notes

Printing: Scored fold (so it stands easily)

Brand Guidelines

Colors: Yellow (#FEE000) + Blue (#C1E3E8) + Black (#1A1A1A)

Fonts: Nunito 700 (headlines), Inter 400–600 (body)

Photography: 1–2 high-quality product shots (box cover or puzzle completion)

🎯 Success Metric: Sits on a shelf next to other puzzles. Customers pick it up because the visual caught their eye and the text explained the concept instantly. Converts 1 in 20 browsers into buyers.

Brief 3: Wholesale Catalog (PDF)

Overview

What: Professional PDF catalog showcasing all wholesale SKUs, photos, descriptions, and pricing. Sent to approved retailers.

Format: 4–8 pages, 8.5" × 11" (letter size), full-color PDF

Deliverable: Print-ready PDF + design file (Figma/InDesign)

Page Structure

Page 1: Cover

  • Layout: Full-bleed image or solid color background (yellow + blue gradient, or cream with hero product image)
  • Content:
    • Logo (large, centered): Odd Pieces, 3" width
    • Headline: "Odd Pieces Wholesale Catalog" (Nunito 900, 48pt, centered)
    • Subheader: "Premium Mystery Puzzles for Independent Retailers" (Inter 400, 16pt, centered)
    • Year/Season: "2026" (bottom right, light gray)

Page 2: Brand Story + Key Stats

  • Section header: "Why Odd Pieces?" (Nunito 900, 28pt, uppercase)
  • Brand story (3–4 sentences):
    • Explain the mystery concept
    • Font: Inter 400, 12pt, left-aligned
    • Width: 5.5"–6" (leave margin for right-side stats)
  • Key stats (right side, in boxes or badges):
    • Kickstarter funding: "$XXX,XXX Funded" (highlight in yellow)
    • Reviews: "4.8★ from 2,000+ reviews"
    • Bestseller status: "Amazon Bestseller in Puzzles"
    • Repeat rate: "40%+ repeat purchase rate"
    • Font: Nunito 700, 14pt per stat

Pages 3–6: Product Showcase (1 product per page or 2 per page)

Per product section:

  • Large product photo (top, 4"–5" width): Box cover + completed puzzle side-by-side OR just the box if space is tight
  • Product name (Nunito 700, 20pt): e.g., "Snack Attack"
  • Artist credit (Inter 400, 10pt, light gray): e.g., "Art by [Artist Name]"
  • Description (Inter 400, 11pt, max 100 words): 1–2 sentences about the scene/theme. NOT the full unboxing experience — just enough to intrigue retailers and their customers.
  • Specs line (Inter 400, 10pt, gray): "1000 pieces | [Dimensions] | [Weight]"
  • Pricing box (highlighted in yellow or blue):
    • Wholesale: $19.00/unit
    • MSRP: $38.00
    • Case pack: 12 units
    • Font: Nunito 700, 12pt
  • SKU/Barcode (bottom right): SKU number + barcode image (if available)

Design notes:

  • Use consistent layout for each product (same position, same spacing)
  • High-quality product photography (well-lit, clean background)
  • Alternate colors for pricing boxes (yellow for odd pages, blue for even, or rotate)

Page 7: Bundles + Merchandising

  • Section header: "Bundle Options" (Nunito 900, 24pt)
  • Bundle examples: 3-pack, 6-pack bundles (can be different series)
  • Per bundle: Image + description + pricing (wholesale + MSRP)
  • Merchandising suggestions (bottom half):
    • Photo mockup: puzzles displayed on shelf with shelf talker
    • Copy: "Include free shelf talkers with your order. Help customers understand the mystery concept."

Page 8 (Final Page): How to Order + Contact

  • Section headers & content:
    • "HOW TO ORDER" (Nunito 900, 20pt)
    • MOQ: "$150 minimum (12 units first order, 6 units reorder)"
    • Payment: "Prepay first order. Net 30 after account established."
    • Shipping: "5–7 business days. You pay freight."
    • Support: "Free shelf talkers + marketing materials included with first order."
  • Contact section:
    • Email: wholesale@oddpieces.com
    • Website: oddpieces.com/wholesale
    • CTA: "Apply now. Get approved in 2 days. Start selling in a week."
    • QR code: Links to wholesale application form (0.75"×0.75")
  • Footer: Odd Pieces logo (small) + copyright + social handles

Brand Guidelines

Colors: Yellow (#FEE000) + Blue (#C1E3E8) + Cream (#FFFBF0) backgrounds + Black text

Fonts: Nunito 900/700 (headers), Inter 400–600 (body)

Spacing: 0.5" margins, 0.25" gaps between sections

Photography: High-quality product shots, lifestyle mockups, professional retail displays

🎯 Success Metric: Retailer flips through and thinks "I want to carry these." Every page should build confidence that this is a premium, legitimate wholesale partner.
