Post-Purchase Flow Reference

15 emails for people who are already puzzle-curious.

Built for three tracks: self-buyers, gifters, and gift recipients. Voice stays clever, slightly feral, and aggressively anti-corporate. Every sell points toward bundles first, because a single puzzle plus shipping is a tragic use of everyone's time.

Bundle rule: push 3-pack, 6-pack, 9-pack only
Core promise: mystery image, hidden Easter eggs, secret endings
Audience: 30-45, gift-heavy, appreciates weird
