Email Flows & Strategy

Klaviyo Flow Strategy

Post-purchase branching, gift-recipient capture, and SKU-specific bundle upsells for Odd Pieces. This document keeps the mystery alive, segments buyers correctly, and pushes every conversion path toward bundles only.

3 post-purchase audience tracks
8 core send windows across the buyer journey
3x upsell ladder: 3-pack to 6-pack to 9-pack
Goal

Turn Every Completed Puzzle Into The Next Bundle Purchase

Track split: self-buyer, gifter, and gift recipient all need different emotional framing.
Offer rule: always upsell to bundles, never individual SKUs.
Data rule: capture recipient emails via QR / landing page so gifting can become an owned channel.
Flow Map

Post-Purchase Flow

Start every purchaser in a common order-confirmation node, then route into the right messaging track based on self-use vs gifting intent. Recipients enter through QR capture or forwarded gift messaging and receive their own shorter nurture ladder with a first-purchase incentive.

Gift Recipient Track

Recipient Conversion

Customer-facing nurture for people who discover Odd Pieces through a gift. Lead with warmth, weirdness, and a simple 10% first-purchase offer.

Day 0
Welcome + 10% off Subject direction: “you were chosen.” Copy theme: thanks for joining, explain the brand in one line, deliver first-purchase code clearly.
Day 3
Mystery puzzle experience Subject direction: odd, intriguing, playful. Copy theme: what makes Odd Pieces different from regular jigsaws without spoiling reveals.
Day 7
Series showcase + bundle Subject direction: choose your next weird adventure. Copy theme: introduce the line-up, anchor on starter bundle value.
Day 14
Finished? here's what's next Subject direction: progress-based next step. Copy theme: post-completion momentum, 3-pack or 6-pack bundle CTA, no single-SKU detours.

Klaviyo Setup

Trigger / Filter Logic

Use flow entry rules aggressively so each contact gets one coherent narrative. Branch by event properties, tag updates, survey clicks, and recipient capture source.

Post-purchase parent flow trigger Metric: `Placed Order` at least once. Flow filter: exclude cancelled or refunded orders where available; suppress if customer already entered same series flow in last 60 days.
Self-buyer branch Enter when `gift_flag` is false, no gift-message attribute is present, and order is not tagged as gift. Skip if recipient profile has already been tied to the order.
Gifter branch Enter when order includes gift wrap, gift message, or a checkout property / Shopify tag indicating gift purchase. Suppress if same contact also purchased identical bundle in prior 30 days.
Gift recipient flow trigger Form submit from QR landing page or profile property `recipient_source = qr_gift_box`. Optional split by the gifted series if that data is passed in hidden fields later.
Finished yet? split Day 14 email click split: `finished_status = yes` sends immediately to upsell cadence; `finished_status = not_yet` waits 7 to 14 days before next bundle message and skips urgency framing.
SKU-specific upsell flow trigger Trigger from `Placed Order` where `item SKU` equals one of the mapped series SKUs. Add flow filter so bundle purchasers do not receive smaller-bundle offers.
Dependencies

Checklist Before Build

Order data must expose gift-identification logic: gift tag, gift wrap flag, or custom checkout attribute.
QR codes in boxes must point to `gift-landing.html` or its production URL equivalent.
Recipient capture form needs Klaviyo embed / submit wiring later; placeholder form action is acceptable for this page build.
Profile properties required: `gift_flag`, `recipient_source`, `gifted_series`, `finished_status`, `last_series_purchased`.
Bundle product URLs and discount code logic must exist for 3-pack, 6-pack, and 9-pack offers.
Suppression rules should exclude recent bundle purchasers, refunded orders, and active customer support issue contacts.
Creative assets needed: order-confirm visuals, mystery-hype art, series comparison module, bundle value table.
