What It Is

A 500-piece puzzle built around one deliberate change — a change that makes you think.

Each Perspective Puzzle features a complete illustration with one intentional visual difference. That difference carries the artist's message — something they wanted you to feel, question, or see differently.

It's not just art. It's a perspective delivered through a puzzle.

Puzzle Image (After)
