FOR YOUR DESIGNER — PACKAGING SPECIFICATIONS

Not a mystery. Not a game. A perspective — delivered through a puzzle. This brief has everything you need to design the 500-piece Perspective Puzzles box packaging. Specs, colors, layout rules, and the validation checklist.

Product Overview

A 500-piece puzzle built around one intentional visual change. The box shows the "before" moment. The puzzle reveals the "after" — same scene, 15 seconds later, completely transformed.

Before Image
Magical light beam, children floating up, magical energy
After Image
Magic dispersed — just clouds and wind

Back Panel

Headline

"THE PICTURE ON THE BOX IS ONLY THE BEGINNING" — Bold, centered. This is the story setup.

Show the before/after shift visually, not textually:

Colors & Typography

Color Palette

Color Hex Use

Black #1A1A1A Text, callout background

Typography

  • "BUILD IT TO FIND OUT": Bold, all caps
  • Headlines: Bold, sans-serif, readable at arm's length
  • Body copy: Regular weight, short lines (not paragraphs)

Design Rules

✅ Always Do

  • Artwork dominates (75%+). Red is the frame.
  • Make "BUILD IT TO FIND OUT" impossible to miss
  • Show before/after shift VISUALLY on back panel
  • Keep benefits short (bullets, not paragraphs)
  • Use exact colors from this brief
  • Test on actual shelf if possible

❌ Never Do

  • Let red compete with artwork
  • Explain the perspective shift with text
  • Use corporate language or tone
  • Make benefits long paragraphs
  • Deviate from the colors provided
  • Make the design generic or safe

Validation Checklist

  • Artwork takes 75%+ of front panel
  • "BUILD IT TO FIND OUT" is bold and prominent
  • Back panel shows before/after comparison visually
  • Colors match: red #d32f2f, yellow #FEE000
  • Benefits are short bullets, not essays
  • Layout feels intentional and premium
  • Callout placement is top-right and visible
  • Artist credit is small but readable
