Perspective Puzzles

500pc Packaging Design Brief

Not a mystery. Not a game. A perspective — delivered through a puzzle.

The box shows one moment. The puzzle reveals the moment that comes after — one intentional visual change that reframes everything. This brief tells your designer exactly what to build.

The Product

A 500-piece puzzle built around a single intentional change. What's on the box is the "before." What you build is the "after." The artist's perspective delivered through the puzzle experience.

Before Image
Magical light beam, children floating up, magical energy beaming down
After Image
Same scene — magic dispersed, just clouds and wind

Front Panel (What The Designer Needs to Know)

Artwork

  • 75–80% of panel — Artwork dominates. Red is the frame, not the hero.
  • The before image (magical light) takes center stage
  • Premium, display-worthy illustration

Callout

  • Text: "BUILD IT TO FIND OUT"
  • Position: Top-right corner
  • Style: Bold, all caps, white text on dark semi-transparent background
  • This is what stops people mid-scan. Make it count.

Bottom Section

  • "500 PC" — bold, prominent, easy to read

Back Panel (This Is Where The Magic Happens)

Headline

"THE PICTURE ON THE BOX IS ONLY THE BEGINNING" — Bold, centered, premium feel

Visual Comparison (The Key Element)

Don't explain with text. Show with visuals:

  • Left: "BOX PREVIEW" label + visual of the before image (magical light)
  • Center: Bold red arrow "→"
  • Right: "PUZZLE REVEAL" label + visual of the after image (magic dispersed)

This one graphic tells the entire story. Someone glances at the back and immediately understands: this puzzle shifts when I build it.
