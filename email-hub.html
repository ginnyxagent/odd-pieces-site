📊 How to Read This Dashboard:
• Campaigns: One-off email sends. Higher open rates expected (target 30%+).
• Flows: Automated sequences. Even higher engagement (target 35%+ open, 6%+ click).
• Revenue/Email: $ per recipient sent. Compare across campaigns/flows to find winners.
• CTOR: Click-through rate on opened emails. Shows email design quality.

🎯 Optimization Focus: Which flows have low click rates? Test new CTA placements. Which campaigns underperform? Analyze subject lines and send timing.
