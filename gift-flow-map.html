Gift Flow Map

3 customer journeys, email sequences, and data collection points

🎁

Gifter Flow

Someone buying a puzzle to gift

Step 1
Browse & Buy
Find puzzle on Shopify → add gift message → checkout
↓
Step 2
Send Gift Link
Email sent to recipient with gift link + gifter's message
↓
Step 3
Track Completion
Know when recipient solves puzzle, triggered notifications
↓
Step 4
Re-Engagement
Email: "They solved it! Want to gift another?" → repeat buyer
🎉

Recipient Flow

Someone receiving a gift puzzle

Step 1
Receive Gift Email
Get notification + message from gifter
↓
Step 2
Go to Landing Page
Click link → gift landing form (email + puzzle select)
↓
Step 3
Solve & Reveal
Submit → see answer + unique discount code on-screen
↓
Step 4
Welcome Email
Follow-up: "You did it. Series [X] is next."
🧩

Puzzler Flow

Self-purchased puzzle solver

Step 1
Buy on Shopify
Purchase puzzle for themselves
↓
Step 2
Go to Landing Page
Email + puzzle select (self-purchased toggle)
↓
Step 3
Solve & Reveal
Submit → see answer + unique discount code
↓
Step 4
Progression Email
Follow-up: "Ready for harder? Try Series [X]."
