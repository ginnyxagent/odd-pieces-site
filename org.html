🧪 Product Developer — "What We Make"

Core Duties
  • Research market trends, competitors, and puzzle industry gaps
  • Develop new puzzle concepts with Visionary (themes, sizes, difficulty, formats)
  • Source and manage manufacturers (primarily China-based)
  • Request and evaluate samples — quality, fit, finish, durability
  • Design and iterate packaging (unboxing experience matters)
  • Manage BOM (bill of materials) and landed cost per SKU
  • Coordinate with Brand Lead on product photography and lifestyle imagery
  • Own the product development calendar — concept to shelf timeline
  • Negotiate pricing, MOQs, and payment terms with factories
  • Ensure compliance (safety certs, customs, labeling requirements)
  • Collaborate with Ops on production schedules and inventory planning

💰 Finance — "The Numbers"

💬 Customer Service — "How We Care"

Core Duties
  • Respond to customer inquiries across all channels (email, Shopify chat, Amazon messages, social DMs)
  • Handle returns, exchanges, refunds, and replacements
  • Manage Amazon buyer messages within 24hr SLA

  • Escalate product quality issues to Product Dev
  • Build and maintain FAQ, help center, and response templates
  • Monitor and respond to negative reviews (Amazon + Shopify)
