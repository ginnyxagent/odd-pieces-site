Paid vs Organic

The Guide That Stops the Blend

The Problem: You're Blending Them

Your team is treating "paid ads" and "organic posts" as the same category. They're not. They have opposite rules.

Organic: Make Them Care

Build community. Create desire. Make people *want* to follow you. No sale pressure.

Paid: Make Them Buy

Drive conversion. Every dollar spent = moves closer to purchase. No vibe points.

The Blend Problem: When you mix these, you get organic posts that have prices (killing engagement) and paid ads that are too pretty (killing conversion). Your paid ads underperform because they're not built to sell.

The Core Rules

Organic Content Must:

  • Never mention price
  • Never say "Shop now"
  • Make people want to follow
  • Work without a CTA
  • Be worth sharing
  • Feel like entertainment

Paid Ads Must:

  • Always show price
  • Always have a CTA
  • Start with a hook
  • Work on mute
  • Feel like persuasion
  • Link directly to product

Quick Check: Is This Paid or Organic?

Ask Yourself (In Order):

  • Does it show a price? → PAID ✅
  • Does it have "Shop Now" / "Order"? → PAID ✅
  • Is the goal sales or website traffic? → PAID ✅
  • Does it create urgency ("Only X left," countdown)? → PAID ✅
  • Does it feature reviews or star ratings? → PAID ✅
  • Would a stranger enjoy this without your brand? → ORGANIC ✅
  • Could this go viral because it's entertaining? → ORGANIC ✅

If you answered "yes" to any of the first 5 → it's a paid ad.
If yes to the last 2 → it's organic.

Common Blend Mistakes

❌ "The Organic Post with a Price Tag"

[Beautiful timelapse, cozy vibes] + "Get yours for $34.99 at oddpieces.com"

Result: URL kills organic reach. Price kills engagement. You get neither organic reach NOR conversion.

❌ "The Paid Ad That's Too Artistic"

[Slow-mo aesthetic footage, no text overlay, no price] CTA: "Learn More"

Result: Viewers think "pretty" and scroll. You paid for impressions that don't convert.

❌ "The Tease Series That Breaks for Sales"

5-day mystery tease (organic, great!) → Day 3 caption adds "Pre-order now"

Result: You killed the mystery. Momentum dies. Comments go silent.

❌ "The UGC Ad Without the Sell"

[Customer testimonial, beautiful, 45 seconds] + No price, no CTA, no link

Result: Great testimonial, zero conversions. You paid for feelings, not sales.

The Fix

Choose one lane:

  • Organic: Remove price, remove URL, remove CTA. Make it worth following.
  • Paid: Add price, add CTA, add hook. Make it worth clicking.

Never try to do both in one post. It fails at both.
