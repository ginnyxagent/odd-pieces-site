03

The Pattern

Brand Texture & Signature Background

A repeating, geometric texture that feels uniquely Odd Pieces. Contains puzzle pieces, warning triangles, question marks, exclamation marks, magnifying glasses, timers, and the letter P. Two colorways: dark (black bg, light icons) and light (cream bg, dark icons). Used on tissue paper, social backgrounds, emails, and website sections.

Usage Rules: Dark version: 15% icon opacity on black Light version: 8% icon opacity on cream Scale as needed, maintains geometric quality Typically 80px tile size Works as wallpaper or accent sections Never use as primary content background

The Frame

Content Container Treatment

Typography Hierarchy

Font Scale & Usage in Context

Nunito (800-900) for headlines and labels only. Inter (400-600) for all body copy. Hero headlines feature white text with black outline and hard shadow. This creates maximum legibility and brand personality across all sizes.

Usage Rules: Nunito: Headlines, labels, CTAs only Inter: Body, captions, copy Hero effects: white + 2-3px black stroke + 4px shadow Line height: 1.7 minimum for body Letter spacing: 0.15em for caps labels Maintain hierarchy at all sizes

06

Color System

Color Combinations in Real Use

Six colors work together in specific combinations, never bumblebee mode (yellow on black in large areas). Primary combination: blue + black + yellow. Secondary: cream + black + orange. Dark mode uses blue accents on black. Alert moments use red-orange.

Usage Rules: Primary: Blue bg + Black text + Yellow accents Secondary: Cream bg + Black text + Orange accents Dark: Black bg + White text + Blue accents Alert: Red-Orange bg + White text NEVER: Yellow bg + Black text (bumblebee) Test all combos at 12pt minimum for accessibility
Blue + Black + Yellow
Primary Signature
This is your hero combination. Blue background with black headlines and yellow accents. Use for major announcements, hero sections, and moments that demand attention.
Cream + Black + Orange
Secondary Signature
Cream background with black text and orange accents. Use for secondary CTAs, supporting sections, and content that needs to breathe while maintaining brand presence.
Black + White + Blue
Dark Mode
Black background with white text and blue accents. Perfect for night-mode content, premium dark emails, and late-night discovery. No yellow allowed here.
Red-Orange Alert
Urgent & Alert Moments
Red-orange background with white text and black accents. Reserved for urgent announcements, spoiler warnings, limited-time offers, and moments that demand immediate attention.
