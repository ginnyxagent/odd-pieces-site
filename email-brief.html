Master Email Brief

Odd Pieces · Updated March 2026

Brand in One Line

1000-piece mystery puzzles for people who want something genuinely interesting — not your grandma's puzzle.

Voice

Clever. Curious. A little playful. Never cheesy, never corporate, never boring.

Audience

30–45 year olds. Creative types, gift-buyers, people who appreciate good design and weird ideas. They don't want to be sold to — they want to be delighted.

Don'ts

  • No exclamation point spam
  • No generic puzzle copy ("hours of fun for the whole family!")
  • No stock-photo energy in language
  • Never sound like Amazon or a discount store

Visual Tone

Odd, unique, high-design. Think art book meets puzzle box.
