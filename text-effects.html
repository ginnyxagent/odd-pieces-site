The verdict

Use Always
Hard shadow (4px, no blur) on display headlines. This is the Odd Pieces signature move. Blue or cream backgrounds. Shadow colour rotates: black, orange, or red.
Use Sparingly
Outline or filled+outlined mix — once per layout maximum. Reserve it for the biggest moment on the page: box cover, hero banner, campaign poster.
Never Use
Soft blurred shadow. On anything. Ever. That's the rule. Not a guideline.
